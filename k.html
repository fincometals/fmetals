Specs

Used in various industries such as but not limited to jewelry, dental, optical and various electronic fields. Can be used to weld a variety of metals including gold, silver, platinum and palladium.

  • Model: FM-90j; FM-110j
  • Maximum Single Pulse: 90J; 110j
  • Laser Wavelength: 1064 nm
  • Pulse Width: 0.1-20ms
  • Laser Type: Nd:YAG
  • Spot Diameter: 0.1-2.0mm
  • Laser Welding Frequency: 0-50Hz
  • Cooling: Water Cooled (4 liter capacity tank) Built for continuous operation
  • Observation System: 10x Microscope / HD-CCD (optional)
  • Protective Gas Port: Argon
  • Peak Power Consumption: <=6Kw; <=8Kw
  • Available in both 110v or 220v applications. Please specify your preference when placing your order.
