About us

With over 30 years and two generations of industry mastery, Finco Metals delivers world-class custom jewelry manufacturing. Merging traditional craftsmanship with advanced CAD/CAM technology, we produce exceptional, one-of-a-kind pieces with unmatched precision.

From casting and stone setting to complete A–Z production, we partner with retail jewelers nationwide to bring their visions to life—proudly 100% made in the USA.

Our promise is simple: excellence, reliability, and artistry. Whether you're a growing retailer or an established brand, we ensure a seamless, high-quality experience from concept to finish.
